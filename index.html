George Petrides is a New York-based monumental figurative sculptor whose work spans public commissions, traveling exhibitions, and private collections across Europe, the Americas, and the Middle East.

"Globally-renowned sculptor George Petrides carves a 'matriarchal gaze' into Greek history, weaving together personal memory, ancient craftsmanship and modern innovation." — Forbes Magazine
